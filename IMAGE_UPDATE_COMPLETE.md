## Troubleshooting

### Images not showing?

- Wait 2-3 minutes for Vercel to build
- Hard refresh: `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
- Check: https://vercel.com/dashboard for deployment status

### Colors look different?

- SVG colors may vary slightly by monitor
- This is normal and acceptable
- Images should look good on all screens

### Want to change an image?

1. Edit `scripts/generate-project-images.js`
2. Change colors in the `projects` array
3. Run: `node scripts/generate-project-images.js`
4. Commit and push
